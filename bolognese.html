Bolognese

Description

It's tomatoes, it's spaghetti, it takes four bastard hours to simmer, but at least you can freeze it for a nice meal another day too?

Ingredients

  • 2 Tins of Peeled Plum Tomatoes
  • Half a Pack of Mushrooms
  • 1-2 Onion(s)
  • 500g Mince
  • Green Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Parsley

Method

  1. Fry mince in pan
  2. Throw in onions and Mushrooms
  3. Throw in the rest
  4. Tomatoes and herbs, cartouche, lid on, simmer on low for as long as you dare
